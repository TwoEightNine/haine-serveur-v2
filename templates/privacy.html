Privacy Policy for Haine Messenger

The privacy of our visitors to Haine is important to us. At Haine, we recognize that privacy of your personal information is important. Here is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use Haine, and how we safeguard your information. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Authorization key

We need to obtain authorization key (so-called token) to get the ability to perform all actions in this app. The token is stored in internal storage of your device and never being sent to third parties.

Key exchange

During key exchanges private keys are never being written on a storage and never being left the app. After using all the private keys are being destroyed. The app and the server may only keep the public keys.

Read/write external storage

Ability of reading/writing data is needed to allow you send photos in instant messages and save photos from Haine or store temporary files like stickers. Be sure that accessing to your data is not performed in any other cases.
